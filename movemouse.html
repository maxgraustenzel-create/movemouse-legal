MoveMouse

Turn your Android phone into a wireless Bluetooth mouse and keyboard for your PC.

Get it on Google Play

Features

Touchpad

Use your phone screen as a precision trackpad. Single and two-finger gestures for clicks and scrolling.

Keyboard

Full custom keyboard with 13 international layouts, special keys, and native Android keyboard support.

Air Mouse

Tilt your phone to move the cursor. Adjustable sensitivity and dead zone for precise control.

Macro Recording

Record and replay sequences of mouse and keyboard actions. Loop playback with configurable timing.

Random Movement

Keep your PC active with randomised cursor movement. Runs silently in the background.

Special Keys

Full access to function keys, media controls, arrows, and modifier combos like Ctrl+C.

Requirements

Android 10 or later Bluetooth 4.0+ PC, Mac, or Linux with Bluetooth HID support
