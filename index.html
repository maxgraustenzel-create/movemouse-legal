Privacy Policy

App: MoveMouse  ·  Developer: Cool Artifacts  ·  Effective date: 2026-05-16

This Privacy Policy describes how Cool Artifacts (“we”, “us”, “our”) handles information when you use the MoveMouse mobile application (“App”).

1. What Data We Collect

MoveMouse is designed to work entirely on your device over a local Bluetooth connection. We collect and store as little data as possible.

Bluetooth device name (stored locally only): The human-readable name of computers you have paired with (e.g. “Max’s PC”) is saved on your device in private app storage to enable automatic reconnection. The Bluetooth MAC address of your computer is stored by Android’s own Bluetooth bonding system at the operating-system level — not by the App — and is managed and deleted by Android when you remove the pairing.

App settings: Your preferences (sensitivity, dead zone, left-hand mode, toggles) are stored locally on your device only.

Usage session data (stored locally only): To decide when to ask for a Play Store rating, the App tracks the following counters locally: whether a Bluetooth connection was made in the current session, whether you visited the troubleshooting guide, the number of consecutive positive sessions, and the foreground time of the current session. None of this data leaves your device.

Macro recording (RAM only): When you use the macro recording feature, the App captures mouse movements, button clicks, and keyboard input in your device’s RAM to build a replayable sequence. This data exists only in memory during the active recording session and is discarded as soon as you stop the recording or close the App. It is never written to persistent storage and never transmitted.

Support email (user-initiated only): The troubleshooting screen includes a button that opens your email client with a pre-filled support message containing your device manufacturer, model, Android version, and app version. If you choose to send that email, we receive your email address and the pre-filled device information. Your email address is used solely to reply to your support request and is not stored beyond the email thread, not shared with any third party, and not used for marketing.

We do not collect or transmit: your name, precise location, browsing history, or any other personal data.

2. Third-Party Data Collection

Google AdMob: The App displays advertisements served by Google AdMob. Google may collect certain device information in connection with ad serving, including:

• Advertising ID (Android Advertising ID)

• Device type and operating system version

• General location (country/region level, derived from IP address)

This data is processed by Google under Google’s own Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalised ads at any time in your Android device settings under Privacy → Ads.

Google Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Google Play In-App Reviews: The App may ask you to rate it on the Play Store after a series of positive usage sessions. This request is handled entirely by Google’s Play Core library. Any rating or review you submit is subject to Google Play’s terms. The App does not receive or store your review content.

3. Permissions Explained

BLUETOOTH_CONNECT & BLUETOOTH_SCAN (Android 12+): Required to connect to and communicate with your computer as a Bluetooth HID device. Used exclusively for this purpose.

ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION (Android 10–11): On Android 10 and 11, Google requires apps to hold the Fine Location permission to scan for Bluetooth devices. The App does not access or record your location; this permission is a platform requirement for Bluetooth discovery on those Android versions only and is not requested on Android 12 and above.

POST_NOTIFICATIONS (Android 13+): Required to display notifications related to background task activity. The App may show the following notifications — none of which are used for marketing or tracking:

• Background task notification (persistent): Shown while random movement or loop playback is actively running in the background. It displays the feature name and the connected device, and includes a Stop button to end the task without reopening the App.

• Task stopped notification: Shown once when the App is removed from recent apps while a background task was running, to inform you that the task has ended.

• Task interrupted notification: Shown once if the system terminates a running background task due to memory pressure, prompting you to reopen the App.

WAKE_LOCK: Acquired during macro playback to prevent the CPU from sleeping mid-replay. Released immediately when playback ends or the connection is lost.

4. No Accounts, No Cloud

The App does not require you to create an account. No data is uploaded to any server operated by Cool Artifacts. There is no cloud synchronisation.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

All data stored by the App (device name, settings, session counters) is held in Android’s private app storage on your device. It is retained until you clear the App’s data via Android Settings or until you uninstall the App, at which point all locally stored data is permanently deleted. The Bluetooth pairing bond (managed by Android) can be removed separately via your device’s Bluetooth settings.

6. Data Security

Since no personal data is transmitted to our servers, our attack surface is minimal. Locally stored data is protected by Android’s standard app sandboxing and is not accessible to other applications.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Your Rights Under GDPR (EU/EEA Users)

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have the following rights regarding personal data processed about you:

• Right of access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

• Right to rectification: Request correction of inaccurate personal data.

• Right to erasure: Request deletion of your personal data. For data stored locally, this is achieved by clearing App data or uninstalling the App. For data processed by Google (AdMob), please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy.

• Right to restriction: Request that we restrict the processing of your personal data in certain circumstances.

• Right to object: Object to the processing of your personal data, in particular for advertising purposes (opt out via Android Ads settings).

• Right to lodge a complaint: If you are in France, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the CNIL (Commission Nationale de l’Informatique et des Libertés) at cnil.fr. Users in other EU member states may contact their national data protection authority.

The legal basis for processing locally stored data is the performance of the App’s service (Article 6(1)(b) GDPR). The legal basis for AdMob advertising data is your consent, which you may withdraw at any time via Android Ads settings (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR).
